Your Journey to a PhD

  1. 1 Joining the Program: Enroll in a PhD program.
  2. 2 Finding the Right Guide: Identify a guide aligned with your field of study.
  3. 3 Forming the Doctoral Committee (DC): Collaborate with your guide to form a Doctoral Committee (DC) consisting of four members.
  4. 4 Gaining Clarity on Your Field of Study: Define and refine your research area with the help of your guide and DC.
  5. 5 Extensive Literature Review: Read extensively: academic articles, journals, and papers relevant to your field.
  6. 6 Coursework Planning:
    • Complete two mandatory coursework subjects: Research Methodology and Research Ethics.
    • Propose three additional relevant coursework subjects to the DC.
    • The DC will approve two out of the three proposed courses.
    • Finalize the syllabus for the optional coursework in consultation with your guide.
  7. 7 Coursework Completion: Attend coursework classes and pass the examinations.
  8. 8 Comprehensive Examination: Prepare for the comprehensive viva exam, which includes one external examiner along with the DC members.
  9. 9 Setting Research Objectives:
    • Define three core objectives for your research.
    • Write and publish research papers based on these objectives.
    • Publishing at least two research papers is mandatory before thesis submission.
  10. 10 Preparing for Proposal Defence:
    • Continue reading extensively in your field.
    • Prepare for the proposal defence, involving an external examiner and the DC.
    • Publishing one research paper before the proposal defence is highly recommended.
  11. 11 Open Seminar: Conduct an open seminar for students and professors after publishing your first research paper.
  12. 12 Second Research Paper: Pursue and publish your second research paper.
  13. 13 Submitting the Synopsis: Once your second paper is accepted, prepare and submit your synopsis.
  14. 14 Thesis Defence: Prepare and defend your thesis before the committee.
  15. 15 Thesis Submission: Submit your final thesis for evaluation.
  16. 16 Awarding of PhD: Upon successful completion of all the above steps, receive your PhD degree.
